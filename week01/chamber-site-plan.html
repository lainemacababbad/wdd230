Site Name: South Surrey and Whiterock Chamber of Commerce

The Chamber of Commerce is located in South Surrey and Whiterock in British Columbia. It will focus on the businesses in these areas.

Site Purpose

The first purpose of this site is a networking hub for businesses.

The second purpose is for anyone to go in the site and see what local business are in the area.

Scenarios

  • What local businesses are available in the area?
  • How can I register my business to be included in the chamber?
  • Are there any events for the local businesses of the chamber?
